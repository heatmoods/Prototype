Product Description

Our Avocado Toast is a perfect blend of creamy, crunchy, and savory. We start with freshly toasted whole grain bread that provides a hearty base. The star of the show is, of course, our hand-selected ripe avocados, mashed to perfection with a hint of lime juice and sea salt.

We top it with halved cherry tomatoes for a burst of freshness, microgreens for added nutrients and texture, and a sprinkle of our house-made everything bagel seasoning for a flavor explosion. Each toast is carefully prepared to ensure the perfect ratio of ingredients.

This nutrient-dense meal is not only delicious but also provides a great source of healthy fats, fiber, vitamins, and minerals. It's perfect for breakfast or as a midday snack to boost your energy levels.

Preparation

Our Avocado Toast arrives ready to eat. For the best experience, consume within 24 hours of delivery. If you need to store it, keep refrigerated and consume within the same day for optimal freshness and flavor.
